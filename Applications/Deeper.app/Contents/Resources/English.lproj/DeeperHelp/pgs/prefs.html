Preferences

To display the Deeper Preferences, choose Preferences in the Deeper menu. The preferences window displays three tabs.

You can use the Interface tab to turn on or turn off the drawer graphic effect while opening Deeper's panes. You can also reduce the opacity (immediately visible) of the main window by moving the Opacity slider. Choose Metal or Aqua to change the appearance of the main window. This modification will be turned on after you open Deeper again.

You can use the Behavior tab to display or not an alert message before executing an action requiring the closing of an application. You can also choose to show or hide the Deeper Help, the Log window, and the License Agreement when opening the application. When quitting Deeper, the application displays, by default, a confirmation message that you can choose to turn off. By default, the Deeper Log is deleted after quitting the application, but you can choose to preserve it by unchecking the corresponding option.

In the Update tab, select "Check for update when opening Deeper" if you want to see if a more recent version of Deeper is available. Click the Check button to immediately verify the availability of a new version. Then, you can download it to be placed on your Desktop. The Preferences pane displays the date and state of the last search.

Click on Restore Defaults to cancel your modifications.
