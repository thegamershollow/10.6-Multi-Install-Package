Spotlight paneli

The Spotlight pane allows adjustment of parameters relating to Spotlight.

Options:

■

Indexing the startup disk: You can use Deeper to authorize or prevent the indexing of the startup disk with Spotlight.

■

Search in Dictionary: If you turn off the search in the Dictionary, no Dictionary definitions will appear in the Spotlight search-results.

■

Calculator: By default, you can make simple calculations in the Spotlight search field in the menu bar. You can use Deeper to turn on and turn off this function.

Spotlight's Icon:

■

Show/Hide: You can choose to show or hide Spotlight's icon in the menu bar. This does not affect the indexing itself. If you hide Spotlight's icon and repair the permissions with another utility, the icon in the menu bar will reappear.

Click on Restore Defaults to cancel all modifications caused by Deeper or by another application. After execution, the parameters will be reset, and the display will be the same as when the System and the applications were first installed.
