Important Information

Here is some essential information that you must know before using Maintenance.

Backup your data:

Before using Maintenance, back up your data.

Maintenance is not bug free, and no user is infallible. Back up your data using Time Machine or any third party backup utility.

Verifying startup disk:

Before running Maintenance or Cleaning tasks, it's strongly recommended to check the S.M.A.R.T. status of the startup disk and its system files.

These verifications are essential and can be made when you open Maintenance. Errors in the structure of system files are not uncommon and can have disastrous consequences if they're not repaired.

Computer does not respond:

Maintenance cannot destroy the System.

If your computer does not respond during the verification of the volume, you need not worry. This is an expected and normal behavior! Once Maintenance has finished checking, control will be returned to you.

Personal data removed:

Maintenance doesn't remove your personal data…

...but you must observe certain basic rules regarding the file system. Mac OS X is a Unix-based operating system and has a well defined structure. Your data must be stored in your HOME directory, in the existing Folders (for example, 'Documents', 'Pictures', 'Music', etc..., or in additional Folders that you have previously created in your HOME directory). You should never store your documents or applications in folders that are subordinated to the operating system (for example, folders named 'Library', 'System', or in any of the subfolders of these folders).

Anybody can make a mistake, or handle data incorrectly...

Back up your data regularly!

Updates:

Always update Maintenance and your System.

The last versions generally correct at least some of the problems encountered in previous versions.

Uninstall Maintenance:

To uninstall Maintenance, go in the Maintenance menu and choose Uninstall Maintenance. In the confirmation message which appears, click the Continue button. The application and the components created while installing and using Maintenance will be removed.

To cancel the display of this warning when you open Maintenance, go to Preferences > "Interface", and deselect the "Display important Info" option.
