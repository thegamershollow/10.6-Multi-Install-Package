Cache dei Font

These Cache files allow you to accelerate font loading. Deleting them will temporarily slow the Font display but won't delete your personal collections and can solve problems. This option also deletes the Font cache of Adobe, Microsoft, Quark, and GIMP.

Note: After deleting the Fonts cache, the next launching of GIMP will particularly long, but once the Cache file has been rebuilt, the application will open again normally.
