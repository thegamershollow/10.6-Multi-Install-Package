Error while checking the S.M.A.R.T. status of the Hard Disk

If Maintenance detects a problem while checking the S.M.A.R.T. status of the disk, first verify that the startup disk is ATA or Serial ATA. Indeed, in Mac OS X 10.6.x (Snow Leopard), this technology is not operational with FireWire, USB, and SCSI disks.

If your startup disk is FireWire, USB, or SCSI, simply ignore this alert, and turn off the "Check the S.M.A.R.T. status of the hard disk at launch" option in the preferences of Maintenance (Advanced Tab).

If your startup disk is ATA or Serial ATA, backup its content.
