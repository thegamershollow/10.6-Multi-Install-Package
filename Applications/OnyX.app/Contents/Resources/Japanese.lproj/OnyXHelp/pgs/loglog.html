Log

The Log stores the date, hour, and description of every operation executed by OnyX. You can choose the number of lines to display typing the number in the field then clicking the Reload button. You can also display the file in a window of the Finder and delete its content.
By default, the OnyX Log is deleted when quitting the application, but you can choose to retain it by deselecting the option default.
