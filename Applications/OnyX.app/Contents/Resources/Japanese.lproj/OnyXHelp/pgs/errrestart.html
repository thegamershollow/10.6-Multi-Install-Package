Impossible to restart and shut down from OnyX

After some maintenance and cleaning operations, it is mandatory to restart the computer. After running such tasks, OnyX displays a message asking what you want to do (Cancel, Restart, or Shut down). You can also automate the startup and shut down from the "Behavior" tab of the OnyX Preferences window.

If OnyX doesn't want to restart or shut down:

Open OnyX.

In the OnyX menu, click "Preferences..."

In the Preferences window that appears, choose the "Behavior" tab.

Check the "Force restart/shut down" option.

Warning: The Force restart/shut down option restarts or shuts down the computer without displaying any alert message. If you have opened any unsaved documents, your last changes will be lost for good.
