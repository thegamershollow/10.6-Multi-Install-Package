Error while checking the S.M.A.R.T. status of the Hard Disk

If OnyX detects a problem while checking the S.M.A.R.T. status of the disk, first verify that the startup disk is ATA or Serial ATA (which is the case for most newer Mac computers' internal disk). In Mac OS X, this technology does not operate with FireWire, USB, and SCSI (external) disks.

If your startup disk is FireWire, USB, or SCSI, simply ignore this alert, and turn off the "Check the S.M.A.R.T. status of the hard disk at launch" option in the preferences of OnyX (Advanced tab).

If your startup disk is ATA or Serial ATA, backup the hard drive immediately.
